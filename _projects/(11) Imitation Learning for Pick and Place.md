## Overview

Industry-coupled problem-based learning project sponsored by CMES Robotics, completed from September to December 2025. The work focused on an end-to-end imitation learning workflow for industrial pick-and-place tasks on the Robotis AI Worker FFW-SG2 platform.

## Contributions

- Customized and forked Hugging Face LeRobot and Robotis Physical AI tools for the target workflow.
- Collected and curated 200 teleoperated demonstration episodes for policy training.
- Trained and deployed Action Chunking Transformer and Diffusion Policy models on physical hardware.
- Evaluated robustness and generalization across changing lighting conditions and environmental variations.
